BEING Humans Improv

 
 



Being Humans is a Seattle-based improvisational comedy group, specializing in clap-in improv, as created and directed by Stan Wells. The cast rotates from performance to performance.


Current members include Sam Buchanan, Diana Dotter, Christina Filios, Shawn Kemna, Alfonso Lopez, Jordan Naftolin, David X, Karen Ni, Jon Richardson, P.J. Weisberg, and Elizabeth Woods.
